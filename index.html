Thanks for stopping by and checking out my site. I’m Craig McKeachie. I’ve been a software developer for nearly 20 years at this point mostly on the Microsoft stack. I’ve been a Microsoft Certified Solutions Developer (MCSD) for three generations of technology including VB6 and COM+ and two generations of .NET certifications. In the past, I was a Microsoft Certified Trainer and have always had an interest in teaching most likely because many of my family members taught including my mother, several of my uncles, and my grandfather (in a one room schoolhouse). About 8 years ago, I became more interested in front-end development (HTML, CSS, and JavaScript), usability and user experience after working as a consultant at a few different digital interactive agencies on projects. For the past 4 years, I’ve been immersing myself in the innovation happening in JavaScript development and transitioning into a career as a technical trainer/ instructor specializing in JavaScript and in particular the application framework Angular. I live outside Columbus, Ohio with my wonderful wife and two really fun boys ages 9 and 13.
